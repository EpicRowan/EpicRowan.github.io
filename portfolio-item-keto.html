Keto Tracker

Tech Stack: Python, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy,
APIs: Edam food nutrition

Keto Tracker is a full stack Web application that allows users to create an account, log in, look up the nutrition of foods, and record the foods eaten and the day.

Register and Login

Create a new account and log in to your account to save your entries in the database

Search

Search the Edam food nutrition API for a food's carbohydrate content

Record food and date

After your entries are committed to the database, you can view all past entries with the dates and carbohydrate count
